User Guide
Get up and running
in minutes.
Quotient is a fully offline, browser-based quote and invoice tool. Everything saves locally to your device — no accounts, no cloud, no subscriptions.
Quick Start — 4 Steps
1 ⊞
Step One
Configure Settings
Go to Settings and enter your business name, ABN, address, bank details, and GST rate. This info appears on every printed document.
2 ◯
Step Two
Add Customers
Visit Customers and save your clients. Saved customers auto-fill their address and contact details into your documents.
3 ◻
Step Three
Create a Quote
Go to Quotes → Create Quote. Select a customer, add line items with qty and rate, then hit Save Quote.
4 ◼
Step Four
Issue an Invoice
Once work is done, go to Invoices → Create Invoice, fill in details, save, then click Preview & Print to generate a PDF.
Feature Overview
Dashboard
Your command centre
See at a glance how many quotes, invoices, and customers you have, plus your total outstanding balance across all unpaid invoices. Recent documents are listed for quick access — click any row to open and edit.
Quotes
Create & manage quotes
Quotes use sequential reference numbers (e.g. QT-0001). Set a valid-until date, add line items, and write notes or terms. Status options: Pending, Accepted, Declined. Click any row to reopen and edit.
TipOnce a quote is accepted, recreate it as an invoice — open a new invoice and copy the details across.
Invoices
Bill your customers
Invoices include GST auto-calculation, due dates, and payment status tracking (Unpaid, Paid, Partial). Paid invoices display a rotated PAID stamp on the printed document. Your bank details appear in the footer automatically.
Line Items
Flexible line entry
Each line has a description, optional note, quantity, and unit rate. Totals calculate instantly. Add as many lines as needed with the ＋ Add Line button, and remove any row with ×.
TipUse the note field for part numbers, model references, or labour codes — they appear subtly beneath the description on the printout.
Print / PDF
A4 print-ready output
Click Preview & Print on any saved document to open the A4 preview. Use your browser's print dialog to save as PDF or send to a physical printer. Choose "Save as PDF" in the destination dropdown.
Chrome / Edge tipSet margins to "None" or "Minimum" in print settings and disable browser headers & footers for the cleanest result.
Data Storage
100% local & private
All data saves in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Data persists between sessions as long as you use the same browser and haven't cleared site data. Back up by saving a copy of this HTML file alongside your exported data.
Frequently Asked Questions
Can I use this offline?
Yes — once loaded in your browser, Quotient works entirely offline. The only exception is the Google Fonts used for typography; if offline, your browser falls back to system serif and monospace fonts which still look clean.
Will my data be lost if I close the browser?
No. All data is saved automatically to your browser's localStorage whenever you click Save. It persists across browser restarts. However, clearing your browser's site data or using Incognito/Private mode will not retain data.
How do I set GST to 0%?
Go to Settings → Invoice & Quote Settings and set the GST Rate field to 0. Save Settings. All new documents will show $0.00 GST and the subtotal will equal the total.
Can I edit a quote or invoice after saving?
Yes — click any document row in the Quotes or Invoices list, or click a row on the Dashboard. It opens in the Create/Edit tab pre-filled with all existing data. Make your changes and click Save again to update.
How do I delete a document?
Open the document for editing by clicking its row in the list. At the bottom of the form you'll see a red-tinted Delete button. Click it and confirm. This cannot be undone.
How do I change the currency symbol?
Go to Settings → Invoice & Quote Settings → Currency Symbol. Enter any symbol ($, £, €, NZD, etc.). Save Settings and all calculations will use the new symbol. The preview will reflect this immediately.
How do I back up my data?
Open your browser's DevTools (press F12), go to Application → Local Storage → find this page's entry → locate the key quotient-data and copy the value to a text file. To restore, paste the value back in. Keep this file safe as a manual backup.
Can I customise the quote or invoice number?
Yes — the Reference No. field on every document is fully editable. The auto-generated prefix and counter are just a starting point. You can also change the prefix format in Settings (e.g. "INV-2026-" or "WS-") to match your existing numbering system.
Can I run this on my phone or tablet?
Quotient is designed primarily for desktop use. While it will load on mobile browsers, the form layouts and table editing experience are best suited to a larger screen. For creating documents on the go, a tablet in landscape mode works reasonably well.
